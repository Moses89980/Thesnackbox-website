Our Story

Sweet Cakes Online was founded in 2015 with a simple vision: to bring freshly baked cakes and pastries directly to customers’ doors. What started as a small family kitchen project quickly grew into a trusted online bakery serving Kigali City and beyond.

As a family-owned business, Sweet Cakes Online has always placed love and tradition at the heart of its recipes. Every cake is prepared with care, blending modern baking techniques with cherished family recipes passed down through generations.

Since its founding, the company has expanded its services to include event catering, wedding cakes, and corporate packages. Our team of skilled bakers and decorators ensures that each order is not only delicious but also beautifully presented, making every occasion memorable.

Ownership remains proudly within the founding family, who continue to oversee daily operations and uphold the values of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. With over a decade of experience, Sweet Cakes Online is committed to making every celebration sweeter, one cake at a time.
